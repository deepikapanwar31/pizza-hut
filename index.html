when did Pizza Time first apper in the America?

Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre was an American family entertainment center and pizza restaurant chain founded in 1977by Nolan Bushnell, who was also the co-founder of Atari, Inc. Pizza Time Theatre was the first family restaurant to integrate food with arcade games and animated entertainment.
